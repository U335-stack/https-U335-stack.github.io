Our Story

Novara Resort

Founded in 2024  ·  Pokhara, Nepal

Novara Resort is inspired by the beauty of Pokhara and the warmth of Nepali hospitality. Set beside Phewa Lake with stunning views of the Annapurna range, we offer a peaceful escape where nature, comfort, and unforgettable experiences come together.

Over the past 2 years, we have welcomed guests from across Nepal and around the world, creating memorable experiences in beautiful Pokhara.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create an immersive sanctuary where guests experience the finest in personalised hospitality, sustainable luxury, and cultural connection — leaving every visitor restored, inspired, and eager to return.

Our Vision

To be a landmark destination on the shores of Phewa Lake, where natural beauty, thoughtful luxury, and heartfelt hospitality create lasting memories for every guest.

Our Values

We warmly welcome guests, care for the environment, maintain quality in every detail, celebrate local culture, and continuously improve the guest experience.

Sustainability

Established in 2024, Novara Resort is built on a strong commitment to sustainability—powered by 60% solar energy, sourcing 80% of ingredients locally, and operating completely free of single-use plastics, preserving the natural beauty that surrounds us.
