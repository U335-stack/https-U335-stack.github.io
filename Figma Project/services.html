Resort Services

Everything you need for a perfect stay — all under one roof, available around the clock.

What We Offer

Our Six Signature Services

Every service is thoughtfully designed to meet your needs before you even have to ask.

🍽️

Fine Dining Restaurant

Our three on-site restaurants serve a mix of international dishes and authentic Nepali favourites. The Lakeside Terrace offers candlelit dinners with beautiful mountain views—making every meal special. Breakfast is served daily from 6:30–10:30 AM, and dining is available throughout the day.

Open: 6:30 AM – 11:00 PM
🌿

Spa & Wellness Centre

Relax at Novara Spa, where peace and comfort come together. Enjoy Himalayan stone massages, Ayurvedic treatments, aromatherapy wraps, and hydrotherapy sessions. Our trained therapists personalise each treatment to your wellness needs.

Open: 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM · Advance booking recommended
🏊

Infinity Swimming Pool

Our 50m heated infinity pool opens up to wide views of Phewa Lake, creating a calm and relaxing space to unwind. We also offer a separate children’s pool for families. Guests can enjoy comfortable sunbeds, fresh towels, poolside service, and a relaxing atmosphere throughout the day.

Open: 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM
📶

High-Speed Free Wi-Fi

Stay connected across the resort with our high-speed fibre network. Business guests can also access dedicated high-bandwidth connections for conferences. Secure, private, VPN-friendly access is available in all rooms and common areas.

Available: 24/7 · No usage limits
🚗

Airport Pickup and Drop-off

Your journey to Novara begins the moment you arrive. We offer airport pickup and drop-off services using our luxury vehicles, including electric SUVs, with a personalised welcome at Pokhara Regional Airport. Transfers to and from Kathmandu Airport are also available on request.

Book at least 48 hours in advance.
🛎️

24/7 Room Service

Whatever you need, whenever you need it. Our in-room dining menu has 60 dishes, from midnight snacks to full breakfast options. We also offer same-day laundry, nightly turndown service with locally sourced chocolates, and a wellness gift every evening.

Available: 24 hours · 7 days a week
